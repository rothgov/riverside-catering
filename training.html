Training

We recognise that the workforce is our most important resource. As well as being directly responsible for delivering your service they are often considered to be 'part of the school'.

Staff training and development is a subject we are passionate about. In addition to a host of in house training all staff are provided with access to a range of accredited training activities designed to enhance the service that we can offer to your school.

Food Allergy Training

With food allergies on the increase it is vitally important that all catering staff are aware of the risks to pupils that have food allergies and have the necessary information to ensure that all of the food prepared is safe to eat.

In addition to our own in house Food Allergy course all staff have the opportunity to access an Accredited Level 2 Allergy Awareness training should they wish to do so. Access to this learning tool can be offered to schools at competitive rates.

Coeliac UK

An increasing number of children are being diagnosed with Coeliac disease. In order to ensure that our service can be safely accessed by everyone, in conjunction with Coeliac UK, we offer all staff the opportunity to access an online learning package. Access to this learning tool can be offered to schools at competitive rates.

Environmental

Riverside Catering is committed to ensuring that our activities do not have a significant detrimental impact on the Environment. All of our food waste is recycled by Refood from Doncaster. Link to website.

Refood is a specialist food waste recycling and waste management service that uses renewable energy to dispose of commercial food waste sustainably.

Refood have an Education programme that is accessible to schools to assist with education pupils about recycling.
