Dietary Requirements

It is important that we are given accurate information prior to your event to enable us to meet any individual dietary requirements. Please read through the guide below to assist with your booking.

Vegetarian

All of our buffet menus contain vegetarian options but please check individual requirements with your guests.

Vegan

Guests who are vegans do not consume any animal products or by products including dairy. We can source items suitable for these customers or amend our normal working lunch, please speak to your catering manager.

Gluten Free

Some guests may have gluten intolerance and are unable to digest cereal or grain products. We can provide an alternative meal or source gluten free bread with 48 hours notice.

Lactose Free

For guests that have a milk/dairy intolerance alternative options can be sourced.

Halal

For any guests who require Halal meat this can be provided with 48 hours notice from our usual butcher.

Allergies

We have listed some of the allergies above however, feel free to discuss any other requirements with your Catering Manager.
