Our facilities

Accommodation

Crowden has sleeping facilities for up to 40 people across 14 bedrooms. Rooms vary in size and sleep between 1 to 4 people.

All rooms have storage for clothing and personal belongings and freshly laundered bed linen is provided.

On each floor there is a toilet and shower block including a disabled toilet and shower room.

Disabled facilities

The Centre building is fully accessible. Please contact us to discuss any particular needs.

Equipment

Before your visit we can provide you with suggested clothing lists. We can also supply boots, waterproof coats and wellies. Specialist equipment is provided for all activities.

Meals

Full board, half board and self-catering options are available and our staff can cater for any special dietary requirements, allergies and food sensitivities.

Meeting and relaxation space

The Centre has a lounge and TV room to relax in after a day of outdoor pursuits. The dining room can also be used as a classroom, meeting room or for indoor activities.

Transport

Packages are available with transport for groups within certain areas.
