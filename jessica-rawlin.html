Jessica Rawlin

Senior Administration Officer

Jessica started her career working for a large International Travel Company as a Purchase Ledger Clerk before progressing into the Construction industry as a Customer Support Assistant. Working in a Customer focused role Jessica developed excellent verbal and written communication skills which she used to liaise between customers and a wide variety of external professional bodies.

Since she joined Riverside Catering in 2008 Jessica has been putting to good use over 20 years' experience of working within a Business Support role. Jessica leads our small administration team and along with her colleagues Lorna and Megan is often the first port of contact for enquiries from head teachers, business managers, parents and school staff. Jessica takes great pride in ensuring that tasks carried out by her team are completed in an accurate and timely manner.

Jessica's role in managing the support offered to the operations management team and all of our customers is immense covering functions including invoicing, procurement and budget monitoring, recruitment and selection of catering staff, completing DBS applications and co-ordinating a whole host of other functions all designed to support the service that Riverside Catering provides.
