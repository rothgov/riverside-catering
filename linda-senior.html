Linda Senior

Operations Supervisor

Linda began her career with Riverside Catering 35 years ago as a Relief General Kitchen Assistant and enjoyed the role so much that in 1989 she enrolled at catering college to do her City & Guilds 706/1 which she passed with credit.

Her passion for cooking lead here to secure the post of Cook in Charge at Brinsworth Comprehensive where she continued to develop her skills by completing a Diploma in Cookery. She then worked in various schools across the borough gaining a wealth of knowledge and experience before taking up her most recent position of Unit Catering Manager at Swinton Academy.

For the past 5 years Linda has had a dual role working both at Swinton Academy and assisting Kay Birtles as an Operations Supervisor in schools in the North side of Rotherham. Linda has since retired from Swinton Academy but is continuing to support the service in her role as Operations Supervisor.
