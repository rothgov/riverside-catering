Specialist courses

At Crowden we run courses which enable people to be nationally qualified outdoor instructors.

These include the Single Pitch Award (SPA) and the Climbing Wall Award (CWA). We also provide skills courses in climbing, caving, paddlesport, hill walking and navigation courses.

In addition, we can design and deliver one off specialist courses that meet your requirements. Please contact us for further information.
