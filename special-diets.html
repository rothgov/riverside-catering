Medical and Special Diets

We can cater for children with prescribed medical diets, formally diagnosed food allergies as well as cultural diets and have robust processes in place for arranging their supply and delivery.

In order to ensure that we provide meals that meet with your child's needs we will need to meet with parent/carers/dieticians to discuss individual requirements. Medical evidence in the form of letters from GP, paediatric dietician will be required.

Please contact Riverside Catering on 01709 822312 to arrange an appointment with your local Area Catering Manager.

Email: riversidecatering@rotherham.gov.uk
