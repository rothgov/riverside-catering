Our Menus – Secondary Schools

Current prices (September 2017) - Secondary meals £2.25

Secondary schools operate a café system whereby items can be purchased either individually or as part of a meal deal.

Students can purchase a main meal including vegetables and potatoes and a dessert for just £2.25. We have a wide variety of £2.25 meal deals so there is something to suit a variety of tastes. Sample menus 2017-2018 (provide link).

A selection of hot and cold items are available at breakfast, break and lunch time.

All of our secondary schools have biometric cashless payment systems in operation which provides numerous benefits to pupils:

  • Parents can set spend limits
  • Parents can request balance & transaction history
  • Reduces the need for students to carry cash in the school environment
  • Quicker & more efficient lunch service
  • Free school meal pupils remain anonymous
  • Students allergies and dietary requirements show on till (only to till operator)
  • Quick and easy top ups through revaluation units in schools and online payments (where available)
  • Promote healthy eating
  • Block restricted products for individual users
