Environment

Riverside Catering is committed to ensuring that our activities do not have a significant detrimental impact on the Environment. All of our food waste is recycled by Refood from Doncaster.

Refood is a specialist food waste recycling and waste management service that converts commercial food waste into renewable energy.

Refood have an Education programme that is accessible to schools to assist with education pupils about recycling.
