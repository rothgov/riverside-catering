About us

Situated in the heart of the Peak District National Park in an area of outstanding natural beauty, the Centre is an ideal venue for a residential stay or activity day.

Crowden is host to a range of organisations for a variety of uses. Groups that visit us include primary and secondary schools, Duke of Edinburgh groups, uniformed groups, youth groups, special interest groups, mountain rescue training camps and businesses looking to enhance team building skills. Crowden is also an ideal base for National Citizen Service providers.

Crowden an inspirational place to learn, develop and create lifelong memories
