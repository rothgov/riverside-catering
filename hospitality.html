Hospitality

We are committed to providing the highest level of hospitality to our customers. We provide great food for any occasion, freshly made on-site by our school catering teams. Whether your event is a teacher training day, school prom or breakfast meeting our catering team is available to meet your hospitality requirements.

We use locally sourced and produced ingredients wherever possible.

All of our food options contain choices that are suitable for vegetarian diets and we will cater specifically for food allergies and other dietary requirements.

If our menus do not meet your needs, we will be pleased to discuss a personalised menu with you. We provide a range of food from a simple working lunch to a three course meal should you require.

To continually improve our service we welcome all suggestions and feedback you have on the hospitality services provided.

Buffet - terms and conditions

To help us offer the best service and choice to your guests please use the guidelines below when arranging hospitality. We will endeavour to meet all needs but without pre-arrangement the options may be reduced.

Should you need to cancel an event please let us know at the earliest opportunity to avoid wastage.

A charge may be made if adequate notice is not provided.

These are only a guide and we will try and accommodate any short notice request. This may result in slight amendments to the working lunches and buffets detailed in this brochure.

Our breakfast menu and working lunches are served on disposable plates, should you require crockery please advise at the time of booking.

Our beverage service can be served in either disposable cups or crockery according to your preference.

All costs within this brochure are applicable to the service between 8am and 5pm, Monday to Friday.

Functions outside of these normal business hours must be discussed prior to booking and will, potentially incur additional labour charges.

VAT at the prevailing rate will be added to all external events.

Should your event be held off site at a venue without cooking facilities a delivery charge maybe applicable.

Catering cannot be provided without advance confirmation of a relevant cost centre or purchase order number, for invoicing purposes.

Dietary Requirements

It is important that we are given accurate information prior to your event to enable us to meet any individual dietary requirements. Please read through the guide below to assist with your booking.

Vegetarian

All of our buffet menus contain vegetarian options but please check individual requirements with your guests.

Vegan

Guests who are vegans do not consume any animal products or by products including dairy. We can source items suitable for these customers or amend our normal working lunch, please speak to your catering manager.

Gluten Free

Some guests may have gluten intolerance and are unable to digest cereal or grain products. We can provide an alternative meal or source gluten free bread with 48 hours notice.

Lactose Free

For guests that have a milk/dairy intolerance alternative options can be sourced.

Halal

For any guests who require Halal meat this can be provided with 48 hours notice from our usual butcher.

Allergies

We are able to offer a menu to suit a range of dietary needs, please feel free to discuss any other requirements with your Catering Manager.
