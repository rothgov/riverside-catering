Our staff

Over the last 37 years we have built up a wealth of knowledge and expertise.

Our staff are highly trained, experienced, professional and friendly with the ability to communicate easily and clearly with all age ranges.

Our chief instructor has ACI, AMI, UIMLA and APIOL accreditation and is qualified to run Mountain Training Accredited courses. Our instructors have a wide range of national governing body qualifications. All are DBS checked and undertake regular training.
