About us

Riverside Catering are a well-established locally based team of professional, specialist caterers dedicated solely to providing high quality catering services to schools, nurseries, a respite centre for young people and luncheon clubs.

Each day over 14,000 pupils enjoy the great tasting, nutritious meals we currently supply to schools in Rotherham, Doncaster and Sheffield.

Although working primarily with Local Authority schools and Multi Academy Trusts we can also work with standalone schools.

Our primary focus is on helping our young people build strong bodies, sharp minds and lead healthy lives.

Riverside Catering ensures the food we provide is tailored to pupils needs as we appreciate how getting young children to eat healthily can be a challenge. Our aim is to ensure that each day; every pupil has a positive lunchtime experience.
