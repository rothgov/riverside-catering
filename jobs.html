Working for us

We employ local people many of whom are parents or grandparents of children in their local school. Our staff pride themselves on being professional, friendly, helpful and fully trained in all aspects of catering and food hygiene.

Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council is a Living wage employer with very favourable terms and conditions of employment.

See job vacancies

Riverside Catering is committed to safeguarding the welfare of Children, Young People and Vulnerable Adults and requires all of our employees to share this commitment.

All successful candidates will be required to apply for an enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service Check (DBS).
