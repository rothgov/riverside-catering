Mharie Forrester

Operations Supervisor

Mharie began her career in the food industry by successfully obtaining a  BSC (HONS) Degree in Food Technology. Her first position was as a Graduate Management Trainee for G.W. Padley’s, a leading company in the poultry processing industry. Mharie progressed her career within the company to become Technical Manager.

Mharie’s next post was with Northern Foods as a Raw Materials Auditor, this involved working with suppliers to improve their food safety systems and she became the companies’ expert in allergen controls working with a major supermarket to improve allergen control and understanding.

To progress her career she enrolled at University to study for a Post Graduate Certificate in Education specialising in secondary Design and Technology (Food Technology) and has taught at a Rotherham secondary school teaching children from 11-16.

Following a 5 year career break she joined Riverside Catering in 2017 supporting Debbie Hollis in schools in the South side of Rotherham as Operations Supervisor. This role allows Mharie to combine her passions for food quality and safety.
