Megan Stancill

Administrative Assistant

After leaving school Megan began her career as an apprentice in 2013 working for the facilities services team. During her time as an apprentice Megan successfully completed both a Level 2 and Level 3 Business Administration Diploma.

Following the completion of her apprenticeships Megan was offered a permanent position within Riverside Catering as an Administrative Assistant. Megan is a key member of the administration team helping to ensure the smooth running of our service.

Megan may often be the first point of contact with the Riverside Catering head office team and deals with a range of queries and requests to support our service including arranging repairs to school kitchens and the ordering of equipment and materials.
