Kay Birtles

Operations Manager

After graduating from catering college with a Diploma in Hotel and Institutional Catering Operations Kay started her career in the retail sector at Debenhams working as a Catering Assistant before being promoted to Senior Catering Assistant.

She was then successful securing a post with South Yorkshire Police as an Assistant Force Catering Officer responsible feeding of police and personnel with in the South Yorkshire area including meeting any requirements for emergency feeding.

In 1991 Kay moved to Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council as a Client Officer working for Civic Catering. Kay then transferred to Riverside Catering as an Operations Manager which is the role she currently holds having full responsibility for the catering within Schools to the North side of Rotherham.
