Lorna Drakes

Administrative Assistant

After completing her A Levels at Doncaster College Lorna began her career working for Doncaster Metropolitan Borough Council where she spent 7 years working for the library service which involved not just assisting members of the public to navigate their way around the library and the services it provided but also involved taking part in numerous community events and fund raising for various charities.

Lorna joined Riverside Catering in 2014 and is a key member of the administration team that helps to ensure the smooth running of our service. Lorna deals with a whole host of queries and requests to support our service which include arranging repairs to school kitchens and the ordering of equipment and materials.
