Kim Phillips

Head of Catering & Facilities Services

Kim has over 25 years in senior management positions within different public authorities, providing commercial management of services to internal and external customers.

With professional qualifications in procurement, contract and business management, Kim is able to understand the customer need for commercial efficiency within a public sector environment.

Kim joined Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council in 2005 to lead the soft facilities management provision, with a remit to expand the traded services in this area. This objective has been challenging with the customer landscape changing with the move to Multi Academy Trusts. However, the continuous review of customer requirements and clear focus on quality outcomes as a unique selling point has enabled customer retention and growth.
