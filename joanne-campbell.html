Joanne Campbell

Catering Operations Manager

After graduating from catering college Joanne started her career with Sheffield City Council as an Area Catering Officer for the School Meals Service.

Joanne joined Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council in 1989 holding a number of posts in Social Services and Civic Catering before moving into specialising in the School Catering Service. Joanne has worked at senior management level with Riverside Catering for the last 20 years firstly as the Senior Operations Manager and more recently as the Catering Operations Manager.
